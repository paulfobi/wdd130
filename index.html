Greater Accra

Greater Accra is a region in Ghana. It is the smallest of the 16 administrative regions in terms of area, occupying a total land surface of 3,245 squre kilometres or 1.4 percent of the total land area of Ghana. In terms of population, however, it is the second most populated region after the Ashanti Region. The political administration of the region is through the local government system. Under this administration system,the region is divided into five districts namely; Accra Metropolitan Area, Tema Municipal Area, Ga East District, Ga West District, Dangme West District and Dangme East District.

Whitewater Rafting Website Project

Whitewater Rafting Website Project

Project Description & Purpose

The goal of this project is to create an online presence for a whitewater rafting company. The website will showcase rafting trips, provide essential information such as trip schedules, safety guidelines, and booking details, and help establish a strong, relevant, and useful brand identity.

Target Audience

Characteristics: Adventure seekers, young adults, families, and outdoor enthusiasts interested in whitewater rafting.

Scenario Questions:

  • What kind of information would help potential customers decide to book a rafting trip?
  • What safety concerns might they have?
  • How do they usually find and use websites in the adventure travel niche?

Logo

Use a simple, clean logo that represents the fictitious whitewater rafting company. You may choose from the provided Whitewater Rafting Logos repository or create your own.
